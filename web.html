Web Work

Paterson Industries

Project Overview

Paterson Industries, an oilfield services startup, specializes in installing, assembling, fabricating, maintaining, and repairing mechanical piping systems. Since their inception in 2014 no website had been developed and I was pleased to work with a team to develop a full communications campaign with many assets, including this website.

The goals of this site include competitor differentiation and audience exposure which will lead to a robust client base. The website underwent user testing to optimize user experience and increase conversion rate.

The website style conveys a clean aesthetic with simple headings and dynamic real-world images to attract a humble, salt-of-the-earth customer.

I created an entire communications campaign booklet to launch the client into the next phase of the company’s growth.

UF College of Health and Human Performance

Project Overview

A fully online master’s program in the College of Health and Human Performance was launched in 2018 and a new, dynamic website was needed. The first degree program of its kind in the US, a robust website and style guide were created to attract demographics between 21-30 years of age with a college undergraduate degree in related fields.

Bold colors, fonts, and textures are used to create an active and vivacious personality. Special attention is given to the “Apply” page to easily guide applicants through an otherwise tedious application process. A chat feature is included to increase customer confidence that a human is here to help with any question or need. This site contains all of the information and resources a potential applicant would need to seek information to begin a new career or just to pursue a non-degree academic journey.

Office of Distance Learning

Project Overview

The office of Distance Learning has a long history with UF and has many departments and services to offer online students and faculty.

A modern web revamp was created which appeals to young, tech-savvy students who are interested in pursuing various forms of online education with UF. Dynamic elements were chosen to make multi-dimensional services seem simple and relevant, including sortable service tiles, a testimonial section, and a video header.

UF Health

Project Overview

A new, fully-online master’s program at UF needed a new website, Distance Education in Wildlife Forensics and Conservation. Created in WordPress, this site houses all the information for future applicants need to achieve goals of career advancement in wildlife forensics or conservation.

This website uses highly accessible, bold elements to attract mature students between 25 and 35 years of age with a bachelor’s degree in science. Images and headings were used to appeal to applicant interests including not only images of endangered animals, but diverse students working in labs. Clear contact forms and a chat box are used to reinforce two-way communication and increase consumer confidence.
